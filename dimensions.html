Dimensions of Wellness

Spiritual
Finding purpose, value, and meaning in life, often involving beliefs, ethics, and morals that guide actions and give a sense of harmony and inner peace.
Physical
Caring for your body through regular exercise, balanced nutrition, adequate sleep, and healthy habits to achieve optimal physical health and functioning.
Emotional
Understanding, expressing, and managing your feelings in a constructive way, developing resilience, and coping effectively with stress and challenges.
Intellectual
Engaging in creative and stimulating mental activities, expanding knowledge, improving skills, and seeking lifelong learning and curiosity.
Occupational
Finding personal satisfaction and enrichment in your work or career while balancing work and leisure, and aligning your occupation with your values and goals.
Social
Building healthy, nurturing, and supportive relationships, connecting with others, and contributing to your community and social networks.
Financial
Effectively managing your financial resources, budgeting, saving, and planning for the future to reduce stress and achieve financial stability.
Environmental
Recognizing your responsibility to protect the environment, making choices that are respectful of resources, and creating safe, sustainable surroundings.
Cultural
Understanding, respecting, and appreciating your own cultural background and that of others, fostering inclusivity, and celebrating diversity.
Digital
Using technology mindfully and responsibly, maintaining a healthy balance between online and offline life, and protecting your digital identity and well-being.
Creative
Expressing yourself through artistic or imaginative activities, embracing innovation, and nurturing your ability to generate new ideas and solutions.
